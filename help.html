Impact Scrum for Trello Chrome Extension Help

Impact Scrum for Trello is a Chrome Extension that adds a little bit of functionality to Trello.com to make it perfect to use in SCRUM based projects. Impact Scrum for Trello allows you to add story points to cards and accumulates the story points per list.

Adding Story points to a card

Story points are written in between curly braces in the title of a card. {5} upon an error I want to see an awesome 404 page. Upon saving the card, Trello Scrum picks up the assigned points and shows them as a blue badge in the lower left corner of the card.

Actual Story points are written in between pipe characters in the title of a card. {5} |10| upon an error I want to see an awesome 404 page. Upon saving the card, Trello Scrum picks up the actual points and shows them as a gold badge in the lower left corner of the card.

When you flip a card to add more information to that card, you are also presented with a story point picker upon editing the title. This allows for easier entry. The points shown are shown in the commonly used semi-fibonacci sequence: 1, 5, 12, 30, 70, 150.

Allowed entries for story points are all numbers, decimals (like .5 or 4.2) and the question mark (?) for unknowns.
